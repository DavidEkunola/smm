üìñ Social Media Glossary
Algorithm ‚ñº
The system that determines what content is shown to users and in what order. Each platform has its own algorithm.
Engagement Rate ‚ñº
Percentage of your audience that interacts with your content. Formula: (Likes + Comments + Shares) √∑ Followers √ó 100
Reach ‚ñº
The number of unique users who saw your content at least once.
Impressions ‚ñº
The total number of times your content was displayed, regardless of whether it was clicked or not.
CTA (Call-to-Action) ‚ñº
An instruction that tells your audience what to do next (e.g., "Comment below", "Save this post", "Click the link").
UGC (User-Generated Content) ‚ñº
Content created by users/followers about your brand, rather than by the brand itself.
Shadow Ban ‚ñº
When your content is hidden from discovery without notification, usually due to violating platform guidelines.
Viral Coefficient ‚ñº
The number of new users each existing user brings. Above 1.0 = exponential growth.
